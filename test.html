Welcome from our Pastor

As we continue the celebration of the 50 days of Easter, and look forward to the celebration of Pentecost, the birthday of the Church, may God strengthen you and give you the gifts of Faith, Hope and Love.

May the Lord help you to recognize the gift of your life and all who are in it. Strive to live each day with gratitude in your heart. Peace be with you! Fr David Kowalski
